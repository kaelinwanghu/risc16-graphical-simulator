RRR-Type Instructions
---------------------
ADD   Ra, Rb, Rc	Ra = Rb + Rc
NAND  Ra, Rb, Rc	Ra = ~(Rb & Rc)

RRI-Type Instructions
---------------------
ADDI  Ra, Rb, IMM	Ra = Rb + IMM
LW    Ra, Rb, IMM	Ra = MEM[Rb + IMM]
SW    Ra, Rb, IMM	MEM[Rb + IMM] = Ra
BEQ   Ra, Rb, IMM	IF (Ra == Rb) PC += IMM
JALR  Ra, Rb		Ra = PC, PC = Rb

RI-Type Instructions
---------------------
LUI   Ra, IMM       Ra = IMM << 6
